About Us

SMAIT Software LDA is a leading gaming software company headquartered in the vibrant city of Lisbon, Portugal. With a strong passion for gaming and technology, we have made it our mission to create captivating, innovative, and immersive gaming experiences for players worldwide. Our flagship games, such as Ludo and Jhandi Munda, have become household names among board game enthusiasts, providing endless hours of entertainment, strategy, and excitement.

Founded on the principles of creativity, innovation, and community engagement, SMAIT Software LDA has grown into a dynamic force in the gaming industry. Our talented team of developers, designers, and strategists works tirelessly to craft high-quality games that are not only enjoyable but also accessible to a global audience. Whether it's a classic board game brought into the digital age or a completely new concept, SMAIT Software LDA aims to push the boundaries of what's possible in the world of gaming.

Our Games: Bringing Classic Games into the Digital Age

At SMAIT Software LDA, we specialize in bringing beloved classic games into the digital age. Two of our most popular titles, Ludo and Jhandi Munda, have been played by millions of players across the globe. We’ve taken the traditional charm of these board games and enhanced them with modern features, such as online multiplayer modes, AI-powered opponents, and customizable settings.

Our Ludo game is a modern take on the classic board game, offering multiple game modes, customizable rules, and an intuitive user interface. Players can challenge friends or family members in real-time or play against AI-powered opponents for an offline experience. Similarly, Jhandi Munda, a traditional dice game originating from India and Nepal, has been reimagined with our advanced physics engine, allowing for realistic dice rolls and immersive gameplay.
